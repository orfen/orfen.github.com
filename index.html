Ubiquitous social technology,

For the benefit of the human race.

What do we want?

The introduction of a new model for producing and consuming technology that is founded on socially-upheld principles.

This model demands the manifestation of - what can only be described as - a "Unified Social Operating System" that massively empowers all its users.

Such an operating system will drastically simplify all our lives.

How is it possible?

Actually, it's entirely inevitable!

The Node.js developer community is building this future right now.

Simple & elegant programming changes everything.

This history of Computer Engineering is essentially a story of finding increasingly powerful abstractions with which to manipulate our reality. The story is far from over and the Node community is rapidly approaching an especially exciting chapter.

Who are we?

ORFen is a "community interest company" formed in the UK during the summer of 2012. ORFen exists to enable structured collaboration between people and institutions.
